# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 2, 2026**

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application **DocHub** (also referred to as "All-In-One Utility App" or "the Application") for mobile devices, which was created by the developer.

The Application is a modular utility tool providing PDF creation/editing, image compression/resizing, local notes management, QR code scanning/generation, and WhatsApp status saving.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### On-Device Local Processing (No Personal Data Collection)
*   **Local Processing:** All core features of the Application — including images-to-PDF conversion, PDF merging, PDF compression, page reorganization, image resizing/conversion, and QR code scanning/generation — process data **entirely locally on your device**. 
*   **No Server Uploads:** We do not upload your documents, images, notes, or QR codes to any remote servers. Your notes are stored offline in a local database on your device, and local backup/restore files are stored in your device's local storage.
*   **Media Access:** The Application requests permission to access your camera (for QR scanning and document scanning) and device storage (to read selected files and save converted outputs). This access is used strictly locally, and no media files or camera feeds are transmitted over the network.

To support the free availability of the Application and improve user experience, we integrate third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) which may collect information according to their respective policies:

### A. Google AdMob (Monetization)
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the Application. AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers (like Google Advertising ID) and device data to show personalized or non-personalized ads.
*   **Privacy Policy:** [Google Partner Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
*   **Opt-Out:** You can reset your advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads via your device's Android settings (Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

### B. Firebase Analytics (Analytics & Active Users)
We use Firebase Analytics (a service provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage and diagnostic statistics. This helps us count active users, understand user retention, and identify feature usage patterns to improve the Application.
*   **Data Collected:** Device information, session duration, app updates, and events triggered within the app (such as merging a PDF or generating a QR code). No personally identifiable information (PII) or user content (documents/notes) is collected.
*   **Privacy Policy:** [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
