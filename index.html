Privacy Policy

App: DocHub - PDF & Utility Tools

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

DocHub - PDF & Utility Tools ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account or log in.

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal information
  • Documents
  • PDF files
  • Images
  • Notes
  • QR code content
  • Status media

Offline Processing

All supported features operate locally on your device. Your documents and files remain on your phone and are never uploaded to our servers.

Features Covered

  • Create PDF
  • Merge PDF
  • Split PDF
  • Compress PDF
  • Image to PDF
  • PDF to Image
  • Image Resize
  • Image Compression
  • Image Format Conversion
  • QR Code Scanner
  • QR Code Generator
  • Offline Notes
  • Status Saver

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Advertising ID

  • Diagnostic information
  • Approximate location (if provided by Android)

This information is collected by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal files or documents with third parties.

Data Security

Because all document processing happens locally on your device, your files remain under your control.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
